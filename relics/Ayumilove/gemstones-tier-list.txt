<h2 id="gemstone" class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text darkluplite--border">Gemstone Tier List Guide</h2>
<p class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text">The following outlines the criteria used to determine the various tiers in this list:<br class="wpc--darkluplite-element">
<strong class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text">SS Tier</strong> – Best-in-class gemstones delivering exceptional value across multiple areas.<br class="wpc--darkluplite-element">
<strong class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text">S Tier</strong> – Extremely strong gemstones that excel in specific roles or content types.<br class="wpc--darkluplite-element">
<strong class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text">A Tier</strong> – Solid gemstones with consistent usefulness and good synergy potential.<br class="wpc--darkluplite-element">
<strong class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text">B Tier</strong> – Situational gemstones that perform well in niche setups or early-to-mid game.<br class="wpc--darkluplite-element">
<strong class="wpc--darkluplite-element darkluplite--text">C Tier</strong> – Low-impact gemstones that are rarely optimal but may have limited use.</p>
