# Relic Tier Guidance

## SS Tier

### Wand of Submission

- **Best Use:** The best option for most Arena champions because it counters powerful enemies such as Armanz, especially on debuffers.
- **Good On:** Nais the Shadowthief, Armanz the Magnificent, Odin Faefather.
- **Rating:** Arguably the best Arena relic in the game. Reflecting crowd control both protects your champions from control and controls the enemy.

## S Tier

### Golden Elixir

- **Best Use:** Tanky champions who can get maximum benefit from the healing.
- **Good On:** Geomancer, Urost the Soulcage, Niamhe, Spear of Nyresa.
- **Rating:** A cornerstone for keeping solo champions alive in tough boss fights.

## A Tier

### Aspect of Siroth

- **Best Use:** Champions with strong passives who do not need an active turn to provide value. It also protects champions in Stoneskin from champions such as Georgid who can ignore it.
- **Good On:** Ultimate Deathknight, Freyja Fateweaver, Rotos the Lost Groom.
- **Rating:** Can save a champion in Arena against an enemy such as Georgid who relies on landing the initial kill. The Turn Meter loss is quite punishing, however.

### Scroll of the Unseen

- **Best Use:** Slow, high-damage champions in Clan Boss teams. They must have Veil continuously for the relic to provide value.
- **Good On:** Ninja, Trunda Giltmallet.
- **Rating:** Can provide a strong damage boost in properly speed-tuned teams, but most players should choose higher-tier damage relics that are stronger, easier to use, or both.

### Journal of Necrotos

- **Best Use:** Champions with strong Turn Meter boosts, such as Arbiter, or strong heals, such as Tagoar.
- **Good On:** Senna Amberheart, Arbiter, Tagoar, Apothecary.
- **Rating:** Provides a significant boost to two effects that are broadly useful.

### Molten Deathbell

- **Best Use:** Any champion with the relevant debuffs until better relics become available.
- **Good On:** Armanz the Magnificent.
- **Rating:** Control champions benefit from the additional Turn Meter, and the ACC stat is well matched.

## B Tier

### Howling Guardians

- **Best Use:** A tanky champion on Clan Boss or Faction Wars teams who can help keep a squishy damage dealer alive.
- **Good On:** Krisk.
- **Rating:** Decent for protecting a squishy champion in PvE. The wearer must be tanky enough to survive the redirected damage.

### Compass of Anaxia

- **Best Use:** A high-RES reviver or support champion.
- **Good On:** Ankora.
- **Rating:** The effect is mediocre, but the stats and gemstone sockets are good for high-RES support builds.

### Preserved Aberration

- **Best Use:** Poisoners who also deal good direct damage. It is currently overshadowed by Arachnelixir.
- **Good On:** Nell Blackteeth, Supreme Kael, Balar the Lost.
- **Rating:** Provides a useful damage boost for the niche champions who can use it, although Arachnelixir overshadows it.

### Malefic Talon

- **Best Use:** Filler for any damage dealer who uses C.DMG.
- **Good On:** Starter champions.
- **Rating:** A generic damage-dealer stat stick. It is a useful filler option but nothing exceptional.

### Tempus Sanguinis

- **Best Use:** Champions with powerful Turn Meter reduction.
- **Good On:** Alure.
- **Rating:** A strong increase for Turn Meter reduction. Useful against early bosses such as Spider and Fire Knight and in Faction Wars.

## C Tier

### Revolting Seedling

- **Best Use:** Any champion you are trying to keep alive on a budget.
- **Good On:** Vogoth.
- **Rating:** A filler relic for tanky champions.

### Demonic Effigy

- **Best Use:** Tanky reviver champions.
- **Good On:** Rector Drath, Dawncaller Sabitha.
- **Rating:** Much weaker than initially expected. Revivers are generally very strong and usually merit a stronger relic.

### Steel-Eyed Cutter

- **Best Use:** A budget relic for any ATK-based damage dealer.
- **Good On:** Any ATK/Crit DPS.
- **Rating:** A strong budget damage relic for ATK nukers. Save Malefic Talon for HP nukers and use this for ATK-based nukers when possible.

### Hoarder's Cauldron

- **Best Use:** A budget ACC boost for any debuffer.
- **Good On:** Any debuffer.
- **Rating:** A budget relic for debuffers or anyone who needs ACC. It is a useful filler option until stronger relics become available.

### Vigor Mortis

- **Best Use:** A budget relic for PvE ATK-based damage dealers.
- **Good On:** Any PvE ATK-based damage dealer.
- **Rating:** A useful budget relic for PvE ATK-based damage dealers.
