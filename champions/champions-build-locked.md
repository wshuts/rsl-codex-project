# Build-Locked Champions

## Purpose

This document tracks champions whose builds are currently locked.

A build-locked champion has received deliberate investment and tuning. Before changing gear, blessings, masteries, relics, or other build-defining elements, careful consideration should be given to the impact of those changes.

Build-locked does not imply:
- The champion cannot be rebuilt.
- The champion cannot receive a relic.
- The champion is permanently assigned to a role.

It means that modifications should be intentional and reviewed before implementation.

Examples of build-sensitive champions may include:
- Clan Boss speed tunes
- Arena speed leads

- Siege specialists

- Cursed City specialists

Build-locked status is a workflow designation intended to protect established builds from accidental modification.
